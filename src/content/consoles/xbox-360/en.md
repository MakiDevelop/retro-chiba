Microsoft's second console, **launched a full year ahead of the PS3** to seize the early HD generation. Technically a generation of firsts: the unified-shader GPU, the Xbox Live Arcade digital storefront, the Achievements framework, the wireless controller as the default. But the early-SKU Red Ring of Death failures (over 30% fault rate) forced an emergency warranty extension and a $1.15 billion provision. Despite that, with Halo 3, Gears of War, and the integration of Xbox Live, **the 360 outsold the PS3 in Western markets** — anchoring Xbox's long-term lead in North America.
