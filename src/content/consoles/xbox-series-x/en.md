Microsoft's ninth-generation flagship — **the most powerful same-generation home console** at 12 TFLOPS. But Microsoft's **Game-Pass-first strategy** put unit sales behind the PS5 (combined X+S ~30M against PS5 ~80M). The framing is deliberate: Xbox Series X|S is positioned as a **distribution platform for the subscription service**, not a single-purchase competitor. Halo, Forza, and Starfield all launched day-and-date on Game Pass; the 2023 Activision Blizzard acquisition (at $68.7 billion) brought the Call of Duty franchise into the same envelope, restructuring the console industry's commercial model in the process.
