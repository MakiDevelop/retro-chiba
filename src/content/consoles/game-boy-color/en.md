The color upgrade of the 1989 Game Boy. **Full backward compatibility** turned every cartridge accumulated between 1989 and 1998 into a color title overnight, with zero migration cost for existing owners. Pokémon Gold/Silver (1999) detonated sales again. Nintendo's last 16-color handheld — succeeded in 2001 by the 32-bit ARM Game Boy Advance.
