**The worst-selling Nintendo home console** (13.56M, an 88% drop from the Wii's 101.6M). Three failures stacked: **a marketing disaster** in which much of the public read the Wii U as a Wii peripheral rather than a new generation; **technically awkward** HD output that was already a generation behind the PS4 / Xbox One, collapsing the third-party catalog; and a **two-screen GamePad** concept that was ahead of the software willing to use it. But the Wii U's first-party catalog — Splatoon, Mario Kart 8, Breath of the Wild — **all roughly tripled in sales** when re-released on the Switch, and that conversion arc directly defined the Switch's hybrid mobile-and-home strategy.
