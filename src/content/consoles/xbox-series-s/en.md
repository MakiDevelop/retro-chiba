**The only same-generation step-down console of the ninth generation** — one-third the volume of the Series X, one-third the GPU performance, all-digital, $299 launch ($100 below the PS5 Digital). Microsoft's strategy was explicit: "cheap entry, the subscription provides the software." Hardware margins don't have to add up if the unit pulls a customer into Game Pass. The Series S has also become **the floor that every Xbox third-party title must clear** — developers must ship a build that targets 1440p with 10 GB of RAM, a constraint that some studios have publicly cited as limiting the Series X's ceiling.
