A 32-bit ARM CPU that **delivered SNES-grade 2D graphics in a handheld** — and kept the entire 1989–2008 Game Boy library running on the same hardware. The first GB-line landscape screen with color, plus a back-lit upgrade in the SP. The third-party catalog stands as a golden age: Final Fantasy Tactics Advance, Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow, Mother 3, Metroid Fusion, the GBA-era Pokémon trilogy. The 81.51M lifetime figure includes the GBA SP (2003, clamshell with built-in lithium) and the GBA Micro (2005, ultra-thin variant).
