The opening act of the ninth generation. **The first console with a native NVMe SSD as primary storage** — loading times collapsed from the PS4's thirty-second order to roughly one second, a hardware change that broke an underlying design assumption of every open-world game built since the PS2. The **DualSense controller** — haptic feedback and adaptive triggers that change feel per weapon, surface, or material — became the new tactile standard for the generation. The 2020–2022 global chip shortage and logistics chaos compressed the first two years of sales; the rhythm restored from 2023 onward, with cumulative shipments crossing the PS3's lifetime in 2025 and trending toward 100M.
