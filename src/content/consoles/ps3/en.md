Sony's third generation flagship, **a double bet on the Cell processor and Blu-ray**. The Cell's heterogeneous multi-core architecture was ahead of its time and notoriously hostile to third-party developers; early ports under-performed their Xbox 360 counterparts. The $599 launch price (NA 60GB) cut off the non-gamer audience PS2 had captured. But **the built-in Blu-ray player decided the 2007–08 HD format war against HD-DVD** — Toshiba conceded in early 2008, and home-video format leadership shifted to Sony's camp permanently. Late-cycle exclusives like Uncharted, The Last of Us, and Metal Gear Solid 4 carried the platform commercially.
