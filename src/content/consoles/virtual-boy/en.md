**Gunpei Yokoi's last system at Nintendo** — a stereoscopic 3D concept fifteen years ahead of its time (the 3DS would revisit the idea in 2011), but the 1995 implementation could not deliver. **Monochrome red LEDs only** plus a tripod-bound goggle viewer plus eye strain that compounded after thirty minutes — three compounding flaws produced **the lowest-selling Nintendo console of all time** (770,000 units, 95% below even the Wii U's 13.56M). Discontinued August 1996. Yokoi left Nintendo in 1997 to found Koto; **the WonderSwan was his redemption** — though he died in a car accident before it shipped.
