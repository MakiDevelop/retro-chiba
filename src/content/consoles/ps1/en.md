The consumer-electronics-to-games crossover that ended an era. Led by Ken Kutaragi (the audio engineer behind the SNES's SPC700), Sony used CD-ROM capacity and aggressive third-party courting to pull Square, Enix, Konami, and Capcom out of Nintendo's orbit en masse. **The first home console to cross 100 million units**, and the single product most responsible for converting "console = toy" into "console = entertainment hub" in mainstream culture.
