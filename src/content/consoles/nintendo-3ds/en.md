**The first handheld with glasses-free 3D** — but smartphone gaming (the iPhone four years deep, Android three) had already pulled away a substantial share of the casual-handheld market, and 3DS lifetime sales fell well short of the DS. The **2DS and New 2DS XL variants dropped the 3D feature entirely**, conceding that most players had turned it off anyway. Still the platform of Pokémon X/Y, Monster Hunter 4, and the Fire Emblem revival — *Awakening* (2012) saved a series that had been on the chopping block.
