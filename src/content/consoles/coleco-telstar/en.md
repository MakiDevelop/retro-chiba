The defining product of 1976's **first wave of console clones** — dozens of manufacturers wrapped a different shell around General Instrument's **AY-3-8500 "Pong-on-a-chip"** part, and the Coleco Telstar series outsold all of them. Coleco itself had pivoted from a 1932 leather company through toys; the Telstar series funded an electronic-toys division whose accumulated experience would let Coleco ship the **proper next-generation ColecoVision in 1982**.
