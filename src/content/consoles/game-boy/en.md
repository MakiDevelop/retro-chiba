The opening statement of Gunpei Yokoi's handheld philosophy — **"modest specs, long battery, strong content."** Technically conservative (four shades of gray, no backlight, a Z80-derived CPU) but **Tetris as a pack-in** made the platform instantly the most installed handheld of its era. In 1996 Pokémon Red/Blue reanimated the by-then seven-year-old machine and made it global — **the first wave of the Pokémon phenomenon as a sociological event**. The 30-hour AA-battery life remained unbeaten by any same-era competitor.
