**The first color handheld** — September 1989, the same month the original Game Boy launched, a year ahead of the Game Gear. Designed by Epyx, manufactured by Atari. Technically a generation ahead (4,096 colors, color backlight, stereo audio) but **six AAs for four to five hours** produced the same power problem the Game Gear would suffer a year later, and Atari was being squeezed simultaneously by Nintendo's handheld and Sega's Genesis at the home end. The **third-party catalog stayed thin**, lifetime sales kept to roughly 5% of the Game Boy's, and Atari exited the platform in 1995. **The Lynx was Atari's last handheld.**
