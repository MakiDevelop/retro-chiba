Nintendo's answer to the Wii U disaster was to redefine the hardware category: **a single device that worked as a home console and a handheld**. Pull it out of the dock and the screen goes with you; dock it and the picture appears on the TV; the Joy-Con detach to share with another player or snap together as a traditional pad. **A global simultaneous launch with Breath of the Wild as the pack-in** put the platform on solid ground from week one. Animal Crossing's pandemic-era runaway hit sustained that into 2020. Lifetime sales of **146.65M** make the Switch the second-best-selling Nintendo console ever (behind the DS at 154M) and the third-best-selling home console of all time (after the PS2 and PS4). The Switch 2 took over in 2025.
