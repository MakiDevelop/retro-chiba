**The fastest-selling home console launch ever recorded** — 3.5 million units in the first four days, more than triple the original Switch's same-window pace. The hardware jump is a full generation: an NVIDIA Ampere GPU with DLSS and ray-tracing support, CPU and RAM at roughly three times the original Switch, and Joy-Con 2 introducing a **mouse mode** (the controller halves slide across a desk like an actual mouse). **Full backward compatibility with the Switch 1 catalog** preserves the previous generation's 146.65M-strong installed user base as a direct asset. Early projections place the Switch 2's pace ahead of the PS5's, challenging Wii-tier (~101M) leadership for the ninth generation.
