**The first console with a stylus and a dial-up modem** — seven years ahead of the Nintendo DS on touch input, eight years ahead of the PSP on built-in networking. But Tiger Electronics was a toy company (Furby, talking-LCD pets, Talking Sega tie-ins), and the cross-category move into a real game console came without game-industry experience. The infamous **Resident Evil 2 port** — a console-grade survival horror IP rendered in 200×160 monochrome with crude sprite work — became a 25-year-running internet meme as the canonical example of a port disaster. The platform sold 300,000 units; Tiger exited in 2000.
