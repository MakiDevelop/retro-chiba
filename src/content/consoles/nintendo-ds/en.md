**The best-selling handheld of all time and Nintendo's all-time best-selling platform — 154 million units**, a hair short of the PS2's overall record. Coming out of the GameCube spec-war setback, Nintendo committed for the first time to **competing on interface rather than horsepower**: dual screens, stylus, microphone, free Wi-Fi multiplayer. The thinking that became the Wii and the Switch starts here. Brain Age and Nintendogs converted seniors and casual audiences into Nintendo customers. The third-party catalog was unusually deep — FF III/IV/IX remakes, three Castlevania titles, Persona Q, Phoenix Wright.
