**The best-selling home console of all time.** Sony bundled a DVD player into the chassis at a price that undercut standalone DVD machines by a wide margin, making the PS2 the first DVD player a great many households ever bought — a cross-category trick that pulled the console into non-gamer homes and decoupled lifetime sales from the player base alone. Sony's 2024 PS 30th-anniversary disclosure officially revised the lifetime figure to 160 million, 56% above the PS1's 102.4M.
